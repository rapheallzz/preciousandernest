welcome to 360clinic

We are a reputable health care provider birthed out of a passion of making our community safer by providing high quality, accessible, cost-effective and compassionate patient- centered service as well as providing a work-friendly environment for her staff. 360 degree Clinic is made up of a team of medical professionals that seek to improve your overall health and wellness. We are motivated by giving our patients’ longevity and early detection health problems is just one of the routes we achieve this goal. Our state-of-the-art facility aligns with our vision to be at the forefront of healthcare in Nigeria and beyond. No matter what kind of problems you are having, 360 degree clinic is ready to serve your needs and the needs of your family.
